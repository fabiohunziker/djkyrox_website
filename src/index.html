About me

I’m Kyrox, a Swiss DJ and music producer driven by a deep passion for creating unique and captivating sounds. Based in Switzerland, my journey in music started at a young age, and since then, I’ve been dedicated to pushing the boundaries of electronic music.


As a producer, I’m constantly exploring new sounds and techniques, looking for ways to evolve my music and bring something fresh to the scene. As a DJ, I love curating sets that not only make people move but leave a lasting impact.


Switzerland may be my home base, but music is my universal language, and I’m excited to share it with the world.
